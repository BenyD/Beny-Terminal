## G Album: Photo Album Business Platform

A business website for a photo album company to tell their story and showcase products. It focuses on user experience and aesthetic appeal to engage potential clients.

### Features

- **Product Showcase:** Beautiful display of photo album products
- **Brand Story:** Engaging narrative of the company's journey
- **User Experience:** Intuitive navigation and interaction
- **Visual Appeal:** Aesthetically pleasing design and layout

### Stack

- [Framer](https://www.framer.com) - Interactive design and prototyping tool
- [Figma](https://www.figma.com) - Collaborative interface design tool
- [React](https://react.dev) - JavaScript library for building user interfaces
- [API](https://nextjs.org/docs/api-routes/introduction) - RESTful API integration
